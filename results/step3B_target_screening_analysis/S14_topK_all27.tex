\subsection*{Section~S14. Top-$K$ recall for all 27 targets}

Table~\ref{tab:S9} reports top-100 and top-1000 recall under
LightGBM topology-OOD for all 27 targets in the benchmark,
evaluated for the descriptor family recommended in
Section~3.6 (Rec.) and, where different, for the best family
(Best). Values are averaged across the four topology-OOD
folds. For Layer~C log-selectivity targets, the champion
misidentification rate at $K=100$ ($1 - \text{precision@}100$)
is also reported. The top-$K$ analysis confirms the divergence
between regression accuracy and screening yield documented in
the three representative cases of main-text Section~3.5:
descriptor families that are practically equivalent in $R^{2}$
can differ substantially in candidate yield at finite $K$.
